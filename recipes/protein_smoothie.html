Protein Fruit Smoothie

Description

Healthy and protein-rich Fruit Smoothie to go with any pre/post workout routine.

Ingredients

  • Frozen Fruit Bag
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Protein powder
  • Spinach
  • Milk

  1. Add all listed incredients into the blender

    Add 2 tablespoons of greek yogurt, a handful of spincach, a cup of milk, a cup of frozen fruit, and one scoop of protein powder into the blender.

  2. Blend!

    It may take some convincing and patience, but blend everything until everything has been liquified and blended well.

  3. Pour into favourite cup and enjoy!
