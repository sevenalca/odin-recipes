How to make Fried Rice (Personal Recipe)

Description

A Simple yet elegant meal. Rice, pan fried, along chopped vegetables, scrambled eggs, and soy sauce and sesame seed oil to taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 cooking onion
  • a chunk of ginger root
  • 3-5 cloves of Garlic
  • 2 cups of rice
    • \
  • 1 Red Bell Pepper
  • Butter or Olive Oil
  • soy sauce
  • sesame seed oil

  1. Make the Rice

    First thing to start prepping is to make a batch of rice. Use a rice cooker and keep the rice to water ratio 1:1.

    First thing to start prepping is to make a batch of rice. Use a rice cooker and keep the rice to water ratio 1:1.

  3. Preparing the Vegetables

    In this step, what is most important is to have an onion, garlic cloves, bell peppers, ginger root, and spinach (or other type of greens of your choice) as the main vegetables to chop up. The most fun part about this meal is that you can add any other vegetable to the mix that is unused at home to add to this dish. The sky is the limit!

  4. Pan Frying all the ingredients together

    Take a piece of butter or a tablespoon of olive oil to grease the pan first. Then add the vegetables all at once. Then after the onions look browned, that's when you add the rice.

  5. Sauces

    While the rice is cooking in the pan with the vegetables, add the desired amount of soy sauce to taste, and also a line of sesame oil. Stir with rice and rest of ingredients in the pan until the rice is warm and everything is mixed in well.

  6. Add the eggs!

    Now for the final addition to this meal before it's ready to serve. The eggs! Add as many as you would like (I usually do 3 or 4) and keep mixing until you see that the egg is being evenly cooked throughout the dish. It will be difficult to keep the stovetop clean during this step so it's ok to spill some fried rice here and there depending on how small or big your pan is.

  7. Serve and Eat!
