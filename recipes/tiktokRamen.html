TikTok Instant Ramen Recipe

Description

Sweet and Savory Instant Ramen Dish. Great for when you want a cheat meal without breaking the budget!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Brown Sugar
  • Soy Sauce
  • Red Pepper Flakes
  • everything bagel spice
  • Eggs

  1. Cook the Instant Ramen

    Follow the instructions of your instant Ramen Packet. Generally speaking, boil the dried noodles for the time listed. Make sure they are not overcooked.

  2. Make the Sauce

    Heat up butter on a pan on low heat. Add garlic, red Pepper flakes, brown sugar, and then soy sauce.

  3. Toss

    Add drained noodles into the pan, turn up the heat and mix the noodles in the sauce well.

  4. Cook the Egg

    Push the mixed noodles to the side of the pan and add the egg (1-2) on the empty side of the pan. Cooked to desired consistency.

  5. Serve!

    Place noodles into your favourite bowl and enjoy!
